Privacy Policy

Loom Framing — by JezBlade

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how JezBlade ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Loom Framing mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Process

Loom Framing is designed to operate primarily on your device. The App may access or process the following:

  • Camera & Images: The App uses your device camera and photo library to capture and process framing project documents (e.g., PDF blueprints). Images are processed locally on your device using on-device ML text recognition and are not uploaded to any server.
  • Microphone / Speech Input: The App may use on-device speech-to-text to allow voice-based search and queries within the App. Audio is processed locally and is never recorded or transmitted to external servers.
  • Project Files (PDFs): You may import PDF blueprints or construction documents. These files are stored locally on your device and are not shared with third parties unless you explicitly export or share them yourself.
  • App Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, telemetry, or behavioral data about how you use the App.

2. How We Use Information

Any data the App accesses is used solely to provide the App's core features:

  • Extracting text from construction documents for quick jobsite lookup
  • Enabling voice-based queries for hands-free operation on the jobsite
  • Organizing and displaying project-specific framing information

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third parties.

3. Data Storage

All data processed by Loom Framing — including imported PDFs, extracted text, and project information — is stored exclusively on your local device. We do not operate any servers that store your personal or project data.

4. Permissions Requested

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • CAMERA — To capture images of documents for OCR processing
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — To import PDF files from your device
  • RECORD_AUDIO — To enable on-device speech-to-text input (optional)

All permissions are used strictly for the stated features. You may deny optional permissions and the App will continue to function with reduced capability.

5. Children's Privacy

Loom Framing is intended for use by professional construction workers and contractors. The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Third-Party Services

Loom Framing uses on-device SDKs provided by Google (ML Kit Text Recognition) for document processing. These SDKs operate entirely on-device and do not transmit data to Google servers during normal App operation. For more information, please refer to Google ML Kit Terms of Service .

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
